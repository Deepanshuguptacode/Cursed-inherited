Prologue: The Origin of the Curse

In a village a few hundred years ago lived a powerful sorceress by the name Elira. The rich and powerful "Valor" family had wrongfully condemned her as a witch. Because of that, Elira got executed. Dying, Elira cursed the family that one person of each generation of the Valor should die in a tragic way before the age of 30. Centuries passed, and the curse always took one family member's life.

Chapter 1: Eli, the Last Valor

At present, Eli Valor, 28, is the last representative of the Valor family. He knows that if he wouldn't find a way of breaking it, the curse will be coming for him sooner or later. Eli's father and Eli's elder sister had died at a young age, because of the sorceress's curse. Eli has only two years left to try to find out a way to stop the bloody curse.

[Image of Eli]

Chapter 2: Family Secrets

Eli begins his search for a cure from his family's long-abandoned mansion. Up in the attic, he finds journals belonging to his ancestors who were also searching to break the curse. Among all those journals, one journal catches his eye, The journal written by Seraphine Valor. She'd gotten close to curing the curse, but then mysteriously disappeared at the age of 29. In her writings, she speaks of a "shadow" that followed her everywhere. To Eli, this seemed to be linked with the curse in some way or the other.Eli realizes this shadow might hold the key to understanding how the curse works and a way to stop it.

[Image of family mansion]

Chapter 3: The Shadow

As Eli continues his research, the atmosphere that surrounds him begins to take a dark and bizarre turn. He experiences cold spots within his home, hears whispers during the night, and sees the movement of a shadow in the corner of his sight. The curse appears to be strengthening as his 30th birthday draws near.Desperate, Eli consults an expert in the occult, Ayla, a girl who knows about curses and dark magic. . Ayla confirms that shadow following him is part of Elira's curse.

[Image of shadow]

Chapter 4: The Ritual

Ayla and Eli embark on a quest to discover a way to undo the curse. They figured that the only possible way to break the curse is a special counter-ritual at the place of Elira's execution. For this to happen, however, Eli will have to stand with the shadow and give a portion of his soul as an offering. If the ritual were to be performed wrongly, Eli would have to loose his life Eli realizes that he has no other choice but to take part in this ritual. He begins to prepare himself for the final battle and goes to the place where Elira died...a temple deep in the woods.

[Image of ritual preparation]

Chapter 5: The Final Confrontation

As Eli starts the ritual, the shadow begins to writhe and convulse, its form weakening as the curse unravels. With every spoken word, Eli feels his life force ripped from him faster, more violently than before. His body seizes, collapsing to the ground, wracked with unbearable pain. Blood pools around him as his veins blacken and his skin tears apart. Eli’s screams echo through the air as his body is shredded—every bone fractured, every muscle destroyed. As the curse finally dissipates, it leaves behind only a lifeless, brutalized shell of what Eli once was. The curse is broken, but Eli is gone, his life violently claimed in its final moments.
